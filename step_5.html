Aim for the Jiggle

Time: ~35minutes
  • Spoon your chocolately concoction into the soufflé dish
  • TRICK: run your thumb around the inside edge of the soufflé dish. This will help the cake rise evenly!
  • Place the dish in the middle of the oven
  • Bake 24 - 26 minutes, until puffy and ever so slightly crusty on top
  • Sprinkle confectioner's sugar on top
  • SERVE IMMEDIATELY!
