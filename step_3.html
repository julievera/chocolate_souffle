Chocolatey Goodness

Time: ~10minutes
  • Fill a saucepan with water and bring it to a simmer
  • Throw a metal bowl over top and add your chopped chocolate to that bowl
  • Melt. That. Chocolate. (stir occasionally)
  • When said chocolate is melted, add the egg yolks
  • Continue stirring, the mixture will stiffen
